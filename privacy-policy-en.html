Privacy Policy - Daily Quest

Last Updated: 2025-05-19

1. What data do we collect?

We follow the principle of data minimization. The app does not collect sensitive personal information.

  • Habit and task data: Stored locally on your device.
  • Nickname and avatar: Used for personalization only.
  • Location & Language: Used to auto-adjust language/time, not transmitted.
  • Group features (optional): Synced via Firebase only if enabled.

2. How is the data used?

Your data is used solely to provide app features like task tracking, personalization, and syncing if needed.

3. Do we share your data?

No. We do not share or sell your data to third parties. Group sync data is securely stored in Firebase (if enabled).

4. Your Rights

You may delete the app anytime to remove local data. Cloud data (if any) can be removed upon request.

  • Expo (React Native Platform)
